Our Legal Services

At BM Attorneys Inc., we provide comprehensive legal support tailored to the unique needs of individuals and businesses. Our core practice areas include:

  • Road Accident Fund (RAF) Claims: Providing expert assistance in navigating RAF claims to ensure clients receive fair and just compensation for their injuries and losses.
  • Deceased Estates & Property Law: Handling the seamless administration of deceased estates and managing property transfers, municipal property matters, and related conveyancing services.
  • Immigration & Refugee Law: Providing specialized guidance and representation for visa applications, work permits, asylum claims, appeals, and judicial reviews.
  • Civil & Criminal Litigation: Delivering skilled courtroom representation with a dedicated focus on achieving favorable outcomes for our clients.
  • Family Law: Assisting clients with sensitivity and professionalism in matters regarding divorce, child custody, maintenance, and other domestic legal issues.
  • Commercial & Corporate Law: Offering strategic guidance on business contracts, corporate compliance, and tailored legal strategies for business growth.
  • Labour Law: Supporting both employers and employees in resolving employment disputes, drafting contracts, and protecting workplace rights.
