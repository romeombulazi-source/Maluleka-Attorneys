About Us

B Maluleka Attorneys Inc is led by Mr Bhekithemba Maluleka, an admitted attorney of the High Court of the Republic of South Africa. He graduated with an LLB Degree from the University of KwaZulu-Natal, one of South Africa's leading universities, and specializes in Immigration Law among other areas of practice. With a strong commitment to integrity and professionalism, he provides expert legal representation to clients across diverse matters.
