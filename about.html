Bhekithemba Maluleka

Founder & Director, BM Attorneys Inc.

Bhekithemba Maluleka is the founder and director of BM Attorneys Inc., a firm committed to delivering practical, accessible, and high-quality legal solutions. An admitted Attorney of the High Court of South Africa with an LLB from the University of KwaZulu-Natal, Bhekithemba combines deep legal expertise with a compassionate, client-centered approach.

Areas of Expertise

  • Property & Estates: Expert handling of property transfers, municipal property matters, and deceased estates.
  • Immigration & Refugee Law: Guiding clients through complex asylum applications, appeals, and judicial reviews.
  • Litigation & Advisory: Providing strategic debt recovery, civil litigation, and comprehensive legal counsel for individuals and businesses.

Our Approach

Driven by the belief that justice should be accessible to all, Bhekithemba is dedicated to empowering his clients through expert representation and unwavering professionalism. He prioritizes understanding the unique objectives of every client, ensuring that BM Attorneys Inc. provides both legal success and peace of mind.
